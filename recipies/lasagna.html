Slow coooker lasagna

Description

This crockpot recipe features a savory beef and tomato sauce simmering together for robust flavor. Home cooks love tweaking the dish, swapping cheeses and adding sausage, to match their preference. Allrecipes member Cathy explains, "I will never do another Lasagna in the oven."

Ingrdeients

    1 medium onion, chopped
    2 teaspoons minced garlic
    1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
    1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
    1 ½ teaspoons salt

Directions

    Gather all ingrdients
    Cook ground beef,onion and garlic
    Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, and oregano and stir until well combined and heated through.
    Stir mozzarella, cottage cheese, and Parmesan together in a large bowl
    Spoon a layer of the meat mixture onto the bottom of a slow cooker
